About Us

About us

Welcome to the Delhi Water Bodies Monitoring portal. Our mission is to monitor and protect the water bodies in Delhi. Through this platform, we provide real-time data on the condition of water bodies, allowing citizens to report pollution and contribute to cleaner water bodies.

We aim to create a community-driven platform where users can upload photos of dirty water bodies, participate in gamified activities, and contribute to the overall health of Delhi's water resources. With your help, we can make a difference in preserving the natural beauty and cleanliness of our water bodies.
